#### **4. Game Constants and Parameters**

*   **Player Count:** 2-8 players
*   **Starting Money:** $1,500 per player
*   **GO Salary:** $200
*   **Jail Fine:** $50
*   **Income Tax (Flat Amount):** $200
*   **Luxury Tax:** $75
*   **Mortgage Interest Rate:** 10%
*   **Total Houses:** 32
*   **Total Hotels:** 12
